Week 1 - Part 3: Maths (Arithmetic Operators)

Let's do some basic maths

So now we know how to store data as variables lets do something with our data.
Open up your browser console and type in the following bits of code.

Addition:
4 + 4
Multiplication:
83.25 * 8
Devision:
8888 / 8


The operators we can use are:

  • + Addition
  • - Subtraction
  • * Multiplication
  • / Devision
  • % Modulus (division remainder)
  • ++ Increment
  • -- Decrement
Give a few of these a go
